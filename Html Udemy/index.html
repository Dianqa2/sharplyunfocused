Me and my constant blaberring about wanting to do photography.

I have always been mesmerized with Visual expression. Especially I tend to be completely taken back by what I see in real life. With a camera I hope to, by some miracle, capture what I see and either save it as mine or show it to whomever willing to see.
I don't want to get into a self-deprecating mode and tell you that my vision is in no way special and that my photos are just regular snaps everyone can do. I guess by sharing, I am pushing through some nihilistic walls i build around my art, and I say fuck it, look what I am looking at. I find it cool. Precious gems I'm eager to show others and them understand why optimistic nihilism is a thing. Simply, what I see I feel precious. And I wish to capture that. Ink it into my memory, save it a screen infront of my eyes, feel that exhilaration running through my body.
It doesn't work that way. But, a fraction of it can possibly be captured through various crafts (?) I chose mainly photography, because it's easiest to do (in other words, I lack necessary talent + necessary practice hours in other fields). What I mostly like about cinematography (that is my ultimate dream to tap my fingers into that) is the rawness of colors and sounds. My job is Simply to capture a moment that best transaltes this complex dance of features. I want to elaborate more on what I feel excited to grab the camera and point at my target. Let me show you what I mean.
